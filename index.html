About live data

Couch Spotters checks YouTube for live plane-spotting streams automatically every 2 hours, with a quick top-up check every 5 minutes that drops any stream that's since ended — so "Now Playing" stays accurate without you doing anything.

Flight positions near you and around Melbourne Airport update live whenever you open or refresh those tabs. None of this needs a personal API key anymore — it's all handled automatically behind the scenes.

Data sources: YouTube (live streams), airplanes.live (flight positions), and adsbdb.com (airline & route lookups).
